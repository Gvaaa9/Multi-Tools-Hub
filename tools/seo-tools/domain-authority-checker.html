Understanding Domain Authority
What is Domain Authority?

Domain Authority (DA) is a metric that predicts how well a website will rank on search engines. It's scored from 1-100.

DA Score Ranges
  • 90-100: Excellent
  • 70-89: Very Good
  • 50-69: Good
  • 30-49: Fair
  • 0-29: Poor
Factors Affecting DA
  • Number of backlinks
  • Quality of backlinks
  • Domain age
  • Content quality
  • Technical SEO
  • Social signals
