Understanding Backlinks
Backlink Quality Factors
  • Domain Authority: Higher DA = better backlink
  • Relevance: Links from related sites are more valuable
  • Anchor Text: Natural, descriptive anchor text is best
  • Link Type: Editorial links are more valuable than directory links
Backlink Types
  • Editorial: Natural mentions and citations
  • Guest Posts: Articles written for other sites
  • Directory: Business directory listings
  • Social: Social media profile links
