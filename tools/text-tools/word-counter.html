How to Use Word Counter

  1. Type or paste your text into the text area

What We Count
Basic Counts
  • Words: Individual words separated by spaces
  • Characters: Total characters including spaces
  • Characters (no spaces): Characters excluding spaces
  • Sentences: Sentences ending with . ! or ?
Advanced Statistics
  • Paragraphs: Text blocks separated by line breaks
  • Reading Time: Estimated time to read the text
  • Speaking Time: Estimated time to speak the text
  • Average Words per Sentence: Text complexity metric
