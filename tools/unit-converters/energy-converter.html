How to Use Energy Converter

  1. Enter the energy value you want to convert
  2. Select the source unit (from)
  3. Select the target unit (to)
  4. Click "Convert" to get the result
  5. Use "Swap" to reverse the conversion
Supported Energy Units

  • Kilojoule (kJ) - 1,000 J

  • Watt Hour (Wh) - 3,600 J
