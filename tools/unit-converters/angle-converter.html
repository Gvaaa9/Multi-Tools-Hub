How to Use Angle Converter

  1. Enter the angle value you want to convert
  2. Select the source unit (from)
  3. Select the target unit (to)
  4. Click "Convert" to get the result
  5. Use "Swap" to reverse the conversion
Supported Angle Units
Common Units
  • Degree (°) - 360° = 1 turn
  • Radian (rad) - 2π rad = 1 turn
  • Gradian (grad) - 400 grad = 1 turn
  • Turn (rev) - 1 turn = 360°
Other Units
  • Minute of Arc (') - 60' = 1°
  • Second of Arc (") - 60" = 1'
  • Mil (mil) - 6400 mil = 1 turn
