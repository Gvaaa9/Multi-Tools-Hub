Supported Temperature Units
Common Scales
  • Celsius (°C) - Metric system
  • Fahrenheit (°F) - Imperial system
  • Kelvin (K) - Scientific scale
  • Rankine (°R) - Absolute scale
Key Points
  • Water freezes at 0°C, 32°F, 273.15K
  • Water boils at 100°C, 212°F, 373.15K
  • Absolute zero is 0K, -273.15°C
  • Room temperature is ~20°C, ~68°F
