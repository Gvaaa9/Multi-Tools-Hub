How to Crop Images

  1. Upload your image by clicking "Choose File" or dragging and dropping
  2. Select the desired aspect ratio or choose "Free" for custom cropping
  3. Click and drag on the image to select the area you want to crop
  4. Adjust the selection using the corner handles if needed
  5. Click "Crop Image" to crop and download your image
Aspect Ratios
  • 1:1 (Square): Perfect for Instagram posts
  • 4:3: Standard photo ratio
  • 3:2: Classic camera ratio
  • 16:9: Widescreen, perfect for videos
  • 21:9: Ultra-wide format
  • Free: No aspect ratio restrictions
